Employee/Dependent Tuition

Fields marked with an asterisk (*) are required.

EMPLOYEES initiating their tuition waiver for classes pursuant to OCC Administrative Policies 2.3.3 or a collective bargaining agreement must complete the information in Section I and submit the form to human resources. The form need only be completed and submitted one time, unless your classification changes. (e.g. Part-time to Full-time, Classified to Teamsters)

DEPENDENTS / SPOUSES applying for a tuition waiver for classes pursuant to OCC Administrative Policies 2.3.3 or a collective bargaining agreement must submit this form to enrollment services every semester.

FACULTY: (Full-time and retired) Full-time faculty and their spouses or retired faculty members and their spouse must pay for their classes and apply for a tuition reimbursement at the end of the semester.

ADJUNCT FACULTY: Adjunct faculty members, their spouse and dependents must pay for their classes and apply for a tuition reimbursement at the end of the semester.

Employee Information

Faculty members and spouses and retired faculty members and spouses shall be granted tuition reimbursement upon completion of a course. Faculty members shall not be compensated for their spouses or dependent children enrolled in their own sections.
