WHY WE REQUIRE A SOCIAL SECURITY NUMBER

We collect Social Security Numbers (SSN) for undergraduate applications and financial aid recipients to ensure accurate linkage with financial aid records. The SSN is the key identifier provided by the federal government in ISIR downloads after completing the FAFSA, facilitating efficient processing of financial assistance.
