Tuition Reimbursement Policy/Process:

Faculty Members and Spouses

  • Faculty members and spouses and also retired faculty members and spouses shall be granted tuition reimbursement upon successful completion of an OCC course.
  • See Tuition Authorization for Faculty member’s dependents. Dependent children to age twenty-five shall be extended these benefits. The authorization waives tuition rather than reimbursing tuition.

Adjunct Faculty

  • Adjunct faculty are entitled to receive tuition reimbursement for classes taken at OCC equal to the number of credits taught by the adjunct.
  • The individual’s spouse and/or dependent children are also entitled to tuition reimbursement.
  • Tuition reimbursement will not exceed a family total of 8 credit hours in the fall, winter, or summer,or 24 credit hours per academic year.
  • Class/es must be taken within one year of the semester hours earned.
  • Tuition reimbursement may be requested after successful completion of the course(s) taught and successful completion of the course(s) taken.
  • The adjunct faculty, spouse, or dependent children will pay for the class and are reimbursed upon successful completion of class (A, B, C, D, AU, credit by exam).
  • Reimbursement is for tuition only and does not include any fees.
  • If an “I” (incomplete) grade is assigned, the tuition reimbursement may be requested when the “I”grade is made up.
  • After successful completion of the class, adjunct faculty have 60 days to submit the tuition reimbursement request.

Librarians, Counselors or Academic Support Center (ASC) Adjunct Faculty

  • One credit hour of tuition reimbursement will be granted for every 30 hours worked.
  • Tuition reimbursement will not exceed a family total of eight credit hours in the fall, winter, or summer, or 24 credit hours per academic year.
  • Reimbursement for classes must be requested within one year of the semester hours worked.
  • After successful completion of the class, adjunct faculty have 60 days to request tuition reimbursement.

Adjunct Clinical Faculty Who are Paid by the College

  • One credit hour of tuition will be granted for every 30 hours worked.
  • Tuition reimbursement will not exceed a family total of eight credit hours in the fall, winter, or summer, or 24 credit hours per academic year.
  • Reimbursement for classes must be requested within one year of the semester hours worked.
  • After successful completion of the class, adjunct faculty have 60 days to request tuition reimbursement.

NOTE:To be eligible for this Tuition Reimbursement benefit you must be a current faculty member, current adjunct employee or a retired full-time faculty member.

Current Adjunct - OCC classes:

  • Provide OCC classes load documents supporting credit hours taught for eligibility. The employee, the department administrative specialist or the academic dean can provide this documentation.
  • Receipt of payment for class(s) taken – this can be screenshot from Colleague.
  • Grade report to verify successful completion of the course.
  • If reimbursement is for dependent - provide the last tax return to verify the status of dependents using this benefit.
  • Submit all documentation to the Human Resources, Benefit Department - Vanessa Belcher, [email protected] or Cheryl Blake, [email protected].
  • Note: all refunds for OCC classes are processed on the student’s record

Current Faculty - Non-OCC classes:

  • Signed Tuition Reimbursement Application HR-22.
  • Receipt of payment for class(s) taken.
  • Grade report to verify successful completion of the course.
  • Submit all documentation to Administrative Specialist II
  • Administrative Specialist II will complete Request for Check, have the Dean sign appropriate paperwork and submit all documents to the Human Resources, Benefit Department - Vanessa Belcher, [email protected] or Cheryl Blake, [email protected].
  • HR monitors annual reimbursements, verifies paperwork and eligibility and reviews dependent information.
  • HR submits verified/approved paperwork to Financial Services/AR for credit of tuition and refund processing.
