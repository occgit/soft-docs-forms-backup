Course Substitutions Guidlines

1. This form is submitted by Counselors/Advisors on behalf of the students.
2. The Counselor/Advisor will explain the form, process and discuss the feasibility of the form with the student. Counselors/Advisors do not approve substitution requests.
3. The Counselor/Advisor must confirm that the student’s Program of Study for which the Course Substitution is being requested is on the student’s record. If not, student must complete a Data Change Form.
4. If a course is being used by a student to meet degree/certificate requirements, that course cannot be used in a course substitution to meet another degree/certificate requirement for that same credential.
5. A separate form must be used for each substitution. Approved substitutions are valid only for the program(s) indicated.
6. The student must provide the required attachments as noted on the front of the form. If the material is not attached, the form will not be processed and returned to student.
7. The approving Dean is the Dean of the discipline / program.
8. The Dean's decision on a substitution request is final the decision will be entered in the student system.

CAUTION: YOUR PROPOSED SUBSTITUTION MAY NOT MEET THE MTA (MICHIGAN TRANSFER AGREEMENT) GUIDELINES.
