Mileage

Note to Originator: If available, please select the ‘From’ and ‘To’ locations using the dropdown menus below. If your desired location does not appear in either dropdown, you may enter it manually. In some cases, standard mileage will be automatically calculated based on your entries. If no mileage is returned, please enter the appropriate mileage yourself. Selecting 'Round Trip' doubles the dollar amount for that row. No calculation will be attempted until 'Date,' 'From,' and 'To' inputs are filled.

Reimbursable Mileage: Business mileage traveled on normal workdays will be reimbursed in excess of the traveler’s normal work commute.
Reimbursable Mileage per day = Total actual mileage driven (minus) normal work commute mileage (Home ↔ Office)
