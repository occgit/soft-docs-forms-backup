Sabbatical Leave Application

Applicant Information

Please review the information below along with the applicant's attached sabbatical leave proposal.

To be completed by the applicant: Please fill out, sign, and date this form before submitting it to your supervisor for approval.

Sabbatical Information

Note to Applicant: By entering your name below and submitting this form you are officially acknowledging your request for sabbatical leave.

Applicant must attach sabbatical leave proposal describing project/plan of study, noting areas specified in rubric.

For Completion by HR Department

Note: By entering your name below and approving this form you are acknowledging your review of the applicant's request for sabbatical leave.

For Completion by Department Chair

Note to Department Chair: Please select the Executive Council Member responsible for committee selection. After you approve this form, it will be sent to the selected individual.

Committee Selection

Note to Executive Council Member: Please select up to 10 members to serve on the committee reviewing this Sabbatical Leave Request. Once you have selected the members, click the 'Send to Committee' button below. This will send each committee member an email with a link to view this form and its attachment in Etrieve Content. After the committee has completed their review, click either the 'Approve' or 'Decline' button below the form.

  • If approved, the form will automatically be sent to the Provost for faculty applicants, or to the Provost for OCC staff applicants.
