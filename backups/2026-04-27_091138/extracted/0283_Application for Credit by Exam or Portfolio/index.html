Application for Credit by Exam or Portfolio

For students with prior learning experience, credit for prior learning can help save time and money toward achieving academic goals. Please review the course description and learning outcomes for the course you are considering prior to applying. Reach out to the faculty member or academic dean with any questions about course content.

Note:

  • Students must be degree or certificate seeking and may not earn more than 45 credits via credit for prior learning.
  • The fee is $25 per exam or portfolio review. This fee is non-refundable and only one submission is allowed per course.
  • If the application is approved, the fee must be paid before taking the exam or submitting the portfolio for review. Payment can be made via MyOCC.
  • The grade earned through credit by exam or portfolio becomes the final grade for the class and is added to a student’s transcript and GPA.
  • Credit transferability is subject to policies of the receiving institution. Students who intend to transfer should discuss with an OCC counselor.
