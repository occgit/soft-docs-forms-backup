New Residency Information

NOTE: Current students updating their address must provide one of the following proofs of residency:

  • Driver’s License or Secretary of State Personal Identification Card with cardholder’s current address.
  • Voter Registration Card cardholder's current address.
  • Current property tax receipt for the place of residence.
  • Current lease agreement for the place of residence.
Valid government ID required with form for changes to be processed. You can attach the necessary documentation by clicking the 'Attachments' button in the menu beneath the form before submitting.

Changes in residency status with proper documentation needs to be submitted by the FIFTH business day of the current semester. Changes made after that time will be effective the following term.

More detailed information can be found at this link: https://www.oaklandcc.edu/admissions/residency/.
