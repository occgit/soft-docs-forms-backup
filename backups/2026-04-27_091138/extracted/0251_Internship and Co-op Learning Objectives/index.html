Internship and Co-op Learning Objectives

Fields marked with an asterisk (*) are required.

Developing learning objectives is an important part of your internship/co-op work experience. Learning objectives will help you to focus on what your faculty sponsor and employer hope you will gain from the assignment. Please determine three learning objectives for your internship/co-op work assignment. The Learning Objectives form is due two weeks after starting your internship/co-op . It is the student’s responsibility to complete this form, review with the Faculty Supervisor/Coordinator and obtain signature. We recommend that you retain a copy of your learning objectives to assist you when writing your final report.

Student Information

ℹ️ Learning Objectives Guidance

When developing learning objectives for your work assignment, consider: the tasks you will be doing, what you expect to learn, and how you will learn it.

Objectives must be specific, measurable, attainable, and approved by your Faculty Supervisor/Coordinator and on-site supervisor.

You will be responsible for demonstrating your written and verbal communication skills, as well as your interpersonal skills. When developing these learning objectives make sure to include the ways in which you will demonstrate your skills during the internship/co-op.

Evaluation Methods

  • Employer evaluations
  • Final reports
  • Portfolios
  • Special projects
  • Site visits
  • Journal notations
  • Oral presentations or demonstrations
  • Other methods approved by your supervisor
