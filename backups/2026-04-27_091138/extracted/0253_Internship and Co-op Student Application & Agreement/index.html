Agreements

As you are beginning your internship/co-op job search, please take a few minutes to complete this agreement and make sure you have covered all the requirements necessary for participation in the program. Initial in the blank space preceding each statement and then submit a signed copy to your coordinator for placement in your student file. Good luck and best wishes for a profitable and productive work assignment.
INITIAL WITHIN EACH BOX TO DEMONSTRATE YOU HAVE READ AND UNDERSTAND THIS AGREEMENT.

I have received approval to participate in the internship/co-op program and have submitted this written authorization to my faculty/program coordinator.

I have met with my faculty/program coordinator for orientation to the program, discussing how we will work together to secure an internship/co-op placement.

If I need assistance in my interviewing skills, I will attend one of the workshops that are facilitated by the Career Services Department.

I agree to inform my faculty/program coordinator when I am contacted by companies for interviews, so that pertinent information can be documented in my file.

I agree to register for the appropriate Internship and Cooperative Education course during the registration period for the semester that I have completed this application or the next semester’s registration period. Failure to register will prevent me from participation in the Internship & Cooperative Education Program registration period. Failure to register will prevent me from participation in the Internship & Cooperative Education Program.

I agree to submit a completed Application & Student Agreement Form, Resume and unofficial transcript to my faculty/program coordinator before any company referrals take place.

I agree to submit a copy of my learning objectives approved and signed by my faculty sponsor to my coordinator in the Internship & Co-op Office within two weeks of beginning my work assignment.

It is my responsibility to submit my final report, and my sponsoring company's evaluation of my performance to my faculty/program coordinator. The assignment is due two weeks before the end of the semester, after I have accepted a work assignment. It is also my responsibility to attend any required seminars or meetings that my faculty sponsor or the Program Coordinator may require.

I understand that internship/co-op experiences are training assignments and therefore, I am not by law eligible for unemployment compensation at the completion of my work assignment.

I have discussed my internship/co-op work pattern with my academic counselor and understand how this assignment will fit into my school schedule and graduation date.

I understand that the Career Services & Cooperative Education Office cannot guarantee a placement.

If problems arise on my work assignment, which I cannot handle, I know that I should contact my faculty/program coordinator immediately. (a) I will not quit my internship without first speaking with the faculty/program coordinator. (b) I can only enroll in the internship classes one time.

I understand that in order to enter and continue in the program, I must maintain the minimum GPA required by my academic department for participation in the Internship & Cooperative Education Program and the required GPA of my co-op/internship sponsoring company.

I agree to adhere to all policies and regulations of my co-op/internship sponsoring organization, and of Oakland Community College as stated in the college catalog and required documentation.

I have made my own investigation and assessment of the risks described above, and agree, on behalf of my family, heirs and personal representative(s), to knowingly and willingly assume all the risks and responsibilities associated with my participation in the internship. To the maximum extent permitted by law, I release, hold harmless and agree to indemnify OCC and its trustees, elected officials, officers, directors, faculty, staff, representatives, employees and agents, from and against any present or future claim, loss or liability for injury to person or property which I may suffer, or for which I may be liable to any other person, related to my participation in the internship (including periods in transit to or from my destination), resulting from any cause, including but not limited to ordinary negligence, gross negligence, or willful acts.
