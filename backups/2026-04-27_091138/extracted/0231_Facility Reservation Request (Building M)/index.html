AUDIO VISUAL PROVISIONS

All Computer Labs, Training Rooms and Conference Rooms at M-TEC are equipped with the Smart Technology Package which includes an instructor/facilitator computer station, projection system, digital document camera, VCR, DVD, CD player, Internet Access, and white boards. Requests for additional audio/visual technology or supplies must be made 10 days prior to the scheduled event and are subject to additional fees. Standard equipment will be tested and operational for the event provided that it is listed below.

SUPPORT AND PROPRIETARY SOFTWARE INSTALLATION

Organizations that wish to install proprietary software will be provided one hour of technical support per event. Technical assistance beyond the first hour will be charged at a rate of $50/hour. Proprietary software must be removed by the rental organization from all computers at the conclusion of each complete training session. The instructor is responsible for testing systems with proprietary software prior to class. IT support is available after 8:30 a.m.
