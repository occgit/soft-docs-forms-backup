CHANGE OF GRADE POLICY
A change of grade may not be submitted after one calendar year from the time the original grade is formally issued. PLEASE NOTE

  • An official Change of Grade cannot be approved from a "Blank Grade" to a "Letter Grade" unless proof of registration and payment are attached.
  • All exceptions must have the Academic Dean's explicit approval.
